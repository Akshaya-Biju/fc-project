Fiction

Top 10 captivating fiction books to stir your imagination and emotions.

The Silent Patient

A woman stops speaking after committing a shocking act — can a therapist uncover the truth?

The Midnight Library

A library between life and death offers a woman a chance to undo regrets.

The Night Circus

Two magicians battle in a mysterious circus open only at night.

Circe

A retelling of the myth of the enchantress Circe with themes of power and independence.

The Book Thief

A young girl finds solace in books during the horrors of WWII Germany.

A Man Called Ove

A cranky old man’s life is changed by the unlikely friendships around him.

Little Fires Everywhere

Secrets unravel in a seemingly perfect suburban community.

The Great Gatsby

A classic tale of love, wealth, and tragedy in 1920s America.

Normal People

A moving story about love, identity, and growth between two Irish teens.

The Alchemist

A spiritual quest about following your dreams and destiny.
